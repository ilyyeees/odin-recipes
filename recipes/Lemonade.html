Odin Lemonade


Description:

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup fresh lemon juice (about 4-6 lemons)
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 4 cups cold water
  • Ice cubes and lemon slices for serving

Steps:

  1. In a small saucepan over medium heat, combine the sugar and 1 cup of water. Stir until the sugar is completely dissolved. This creates a simple syrup. Let it cool.
  2. In a large pitcher, combine the lemon juice, the simple syrup, and the remaining 3 cups of cold water.
  3. Stir well.
  4. Serve over ice with a slice of fresh lemon.
